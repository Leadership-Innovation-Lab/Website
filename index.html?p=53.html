Directions

The Leadership and Innovation Lab is located on Penn State’s University Park campus in the Moore Building on Fischer Road.

From Atherton St  – Head East on Park Avenue.  After about 100 yards, turn right onto Allen St, followed by another quick right onto Fisher Rd.  The Moore building will be on your left and the Nittany Deck parking garage is just past it, also on the left.

From the East side of campus  – Head West on Park Ave. Turn left onto Allen St, followed by a quick right onto Fisher Rd.  The Moore building will be on your left and the Nittany Deck parking garage is just past it, also on the left.

Parking

Parking is available in the Nittany Parking Deck, which is located next to the Moore Building. Parking rates are $1.00 per hour. Additional visitor parking information is available here.
